Activities

  • Table Tennis Club at UW-Madison

    The Table Tennis Club at UW-Madison is a group of passionate ping pong players who come together to play and compete. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, there's a place for you in our club. We hold regular meetings and tournaments, and also participate in intercollegiate competitions. Join us to improve your skills, make new friends, and have fun!

  • Cooking

    As a college student, I've recently started getting into cooking. Before, I mostly relied on takeout and fast food to sustain myself, but I've come to realize that cooking my own meals is not only healthier but also a great way to save money. However, I must admit that I'm still very much a beginner in the kitchen. I'm still learning the basics of cooking and experimenting with different recipes to see what works best for me.
