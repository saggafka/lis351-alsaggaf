Personal Information

    I am from Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Jeddah, in western Saudi Arabia, is a cosmopolitan coastal city with a rich history as a trading hub. Its port is one of the busiest in the Red Sea, and its distinctive architecture includes coral houses and traditional souqs. Jeddah's food scene is diverse, and the Corniche offers stunning views and recreational activities. The city hosts several annual festivals and events, including the Jeddah International Book Fair and the Jeddah Food Festival, showcasing its rich cultural heritage.
